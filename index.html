Controls
- Mouse: Take Aim
- W,A,S,D / Arrow Keys: Move your tank
- E: Toggle Auto-Fire
- C: Toggle Auto-Rotate
- F: Toggle Fast Auto-Rotate (3x faster)
- U: Toggle Body Upgrade Tree
- Y: Toggle Weapon Upgrade Tree
- M: Toggle Debug Mode
- P: Passive Mode (you won't deal damage to tanks)
- O: Open Settings
- H: Toggle Hitbox
- X: Spin Lock
- Left Click / Space Bar: Shoot
- Right Click/Shift: Repel Drones
